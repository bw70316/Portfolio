About Me

I initially entered the expansive world of coding envisioning the experience as a great way to supplement my previous writing experience. I imagined myself not only creating the written content for a site, but also developing its layout, design, and functionality. However, as I continue to gain more experience, I realize I have an affinity for coding as a full-stack developer.

As i continue to polish my skills on both the front-end and back-end, I am more and more certain that I have the capacity to develop full-stack applications. Please visit my contact page, if you would like more information about my current projects or simply want to ask a question.
