About Me

I initially entered the expansive world of coding envisioning the experience as a great way to supplement my previous writing experience. I imagined myself not only creating the written content for a site, but also developing its layout, design, and functionality. However, as I continue to gain more experience, I realize I have an affinity for backend development.

When it comes to writing, my creativity is rather expansive. Unfortunately, my ability to paint a picture with words has yet to translate into design. However, I feel that with my current foundation in database management and with continual practice I am equipped with the necessary tools to become an excellent backend developer.
